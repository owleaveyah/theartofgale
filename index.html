About

Olivia Gale Gerochi – also known as Gale – is a multidisciplinary creative and artist based in Iloilo City, Philippines. Painting and drawing are Gale’s primary practices, also used as dominant instruments for expanding into various forms including mix media, multimedia, and handcraft.

She has also delved deep into literary genres such as scriptwriting, creative writing, and poetry as an insatiable reader and writer.

Gale has a passion for telling tales in visual and unique ways. Through concept art, storytelling, and illustration, she seeks to impart depth and meaning. In her work, she looks for the connections between emotions, interpretations, and unreality.
